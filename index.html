Welcome to the Spring Cherry Blossom themed arcade.

Here you can play three games that have a cherry blossom color theme and have a focus on speed and reaction time.

Our hope is that you, the player, are someone who enjoys speed games and want to test how fast you can process things occurring in-game and react with your mouse, keyboard, or fingers!

In terms of accessibility, while all our games are technically accessible via screen reader, they may be even more difficult to play without the proper equipment...
...however, we DID make it possible if you are up for the challenge :D
